## HiveMap — Business Plan

### Executive Summary

HiveMap is a for-profit social enterprise that uses drone-based environmental sensing combined with traditional ecological knowledge (TEK) to identify and activate abandoned agricultural sites for smallholder cooperatives across the Sahel. The platform addresses a $2.4 billion underserved market in climate-adaptive agriculture by providing an end-to-end pipeline from aerial survey to bankable rehabilitation project.

**Ask:** EUR 2 million seed round to fund pilot operations in Mauritania, platform development, and expansion to three additional Sahel countries within 24 months.

### Market Analysis

#### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **28,000 cooperatives** across the Sahel belt (Mauritania, Mali, Senegal, Burkina Faso, Niger, Chad)
- At EUR 4,500 per full pipeline engagement: **TAM = EUR 126 million/year**

#### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- Cooperatives within range of existing NGO/government agricultural extension programmes
- **~8,400 cooperatives**: **SAM = EUR 37.8 million/year**

#### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) — Year 1–3
- Year 1: 15 cooperatives (Mauritania only) = EUR 67,500
- Year 2: 80 cooperatives (Mauritania + Senegal) = EUR 360,000
- Year 3: 250 cooperatives (4 countries) = EUR 1,125,000

### Path to Profitability
- Breakeven projected in **Q2 Year 4** at ~320 cooperative engagements
- Blended gross margin improves from 22% (Year 1) to 48% (Year 3) as monitoring subscriptions and data licensing scale
- Carbon credit facilitation becomes material revenue from Year 3 as rehabilitated sites reach verification thresholds

## Marketing Campaign

### Campaign Name: "The Land Remembers"

### Brand Positioning
*HiveMap is the first platform that pairs drone technology with indigenous knowledge to turn abandoned Sahel land into productive farms — because the most advanced sensor is a beekeeper who has read the desert for 40 years.*

### Target Audiences

| Audience | Channel | Message |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Development funders (GIZ, USAID, AFD, World Bank) | Direct outreach, conferences (COP, AGRF), white papers | "De-risk your climate adaptation portfolio with site-level data" |
| NGOs with Sahel programmes | LinkedIn, development sector publications, pilot case studies | "Give your field teams the tools to find what satellites can't see" |
| Cooperative leaders | Radio (local FM), community gatherings, word of mouth, tablet demos | "Your elders' knowledge + our drones = more productive land for your families" |
| Impact investors | Pitch decks, AngelList, climate tech demo days | "Blended returns: 4x impact multiple, clear path to carbon credit revenue" |

### Campaign Phases

**Phase 1 — Seed (Months 1–6): "Prove It"**
- 5-cooperative pilot with full documentation
- Produce a short documentary (5 min) showing pipeline in action
- Publish pilot results as open-access impact report
- Present at African Green Revolution Forum (AGRF)

**Phase 2 — Growth (Months 7–18): "Scale the Signal"**
- Launch HiveMap blog with field stories and TEK knowledge holder profiles
- LinkedIn thought leadership (weekly posts from founding team)
- Partner announcement press releases (GIZ, university validation study)
- Conference circuit: COP, Web Summit Africa, Slush

**Phase 3 — Expansion (Months 19–36): "The Movement"**
- "Land Remembers" branded content series (video + photo essays)
- Open-source the HiveMap Score methodology (academic credibility play)
- Host annual "TEK + Tech" convening in Nouakchott
- Cooperative success stories syndicated to BBC Africa, Al Jazeera, Le Monde Afrique
